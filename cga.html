Section A: Computer Graphics Concepts

1. Computer Graphics kya hai? [2 Marks]

Computer Graphics ka matlab hai computer ke madhyam se images aur visual elements ka creation. Isme 2D aur 3D graphics shamil hain. Yeh technology use hoti hai films, video games, simulations, aur design applications mein. Computer graphics humein visual representation provide karti hai, jo data ko samajhne aur interpret karne mein madad karti hai.

2. Resolution kya hai? [2 Marks]

Resolution ka matlab hai kisi display par pixels ki quantity. Yeh pixels ki horizontal aur vertical count se define hota hai, jaise 1920x1080. Zyada resolution hone par images clearer aur sharper hoti hain, jo details ko behtar dikhata hai.

3. Persistence kya hai? [2 Marks]

Persistence ka matlab hai ek visual element ka kitne samay tak dikhai dena. Jab ek image ya frame display hota hai, toh woh kuch samay tak visible rehta hai. Iska use animations mein smooth transitions create karne ke liye hota hai, jisse continuity maintain hoti hai.

4. Aspect Ratio kya hai? [2 Marks]

Aspect Ratio ka matlab hai kisi image ya screen ka width aur height ka ratio. Yeh display format ko define karta hai. Common aspect ratios hain 16:9 (widescreen) aur 4:3 (standard). Yeh ratio ensure karta hai ki images aur videos sahi tarike se display ho.

5. Horizontal & Vertical Retrace of the Electron Beam kya hai? [2 Marks]

CRT (Cathode Ray Tube) displays mein electron beam use hota hai jo screen par images create karta hai.

Horizontal Retrace: Jab electron beam ek line draw kar leta hai, toh woh screen ke right se left ki taraf retrace karta hai. Yeh retrace time hai jab beam kisi aur image ko draw nahi kar raha hota.

Vertical Retrace: Jab beam puri screen draw kar leta hai, toh yeh screen ke top se neeche ki taraf move hota hai. Yeh vertical retrace hai, jahan beam next frame ke liye tayyar hota hai.

Section B: Display Technology Comparison

Difference between Random Scan and Raster Scan Display

Feature Random Scan Display Raster Scan Display
Definition Random Scan display images ko directly points ya lines draw karke create karta hai. Raster Scan display pixels ki rows aur columns mein image ko arrange karke create karta hai.
Technology Yeh mostly vector graphics ke liye use hota hai, jahan smooth curves aur lines ki zaroorat hoti hai. Yeh bitmap graphics ke liye use hota hai, jahan images ko pixel-based dikhana hota hai.
Speed Iska scanning speed higher hota hai, kyunki yeh directly line ya points draw karta hai. Isme scanning speed lower hota hai, kyunki yeh poori screen ko row by row scan karta hai.
Resolution Resolution zyada flexible hai, kyunki points ko random order mein draw kiya ja sakta hai. Resolution fixed hoti hai, kyunki yeh pixels ki specific grid par dependent hota hai.
Applications Yeh CAD (Computer-Aided Design), vector graphics, aur animation mein use hota hai. Yeh modern displays, television, aur video games mein use hota hai.

Beam-Penetration and Shadow-Mask Methods

Beam-Penetration Method

Beam-penetration method ek traditional technique hai jo CRT (Cathode Ray Tube) displays mein use hoti hai. Is method mein, electron beam screen ke back se forward ki taraf penetrate karta hai.

Working Principle

  1. Screen Layers: Is display mein, screen par alag-alag layers hoti hain, jahan phosphorescent materials use hote hain. Yeh layers different colors produce karne ke liye design ki gayi hain.
  2. Electron Beam: Electron gun se chali electron beam screen par focus ki jati hai. Jab yeh beam phosphorescent material par lagti hai, toh woh glow karna shuru kar deti hai.
  3. Color Production: Different colors produce karne ke liye, alag-alag materials ka istemal kiya jata hai, jo beam ke penetration ke saath react karte hain.

Advantages

  • High brightness levels produce karne ki capability.
  • Better color accuracy aur saturation.

Disadvantages

  • High power consumption.
  • Color distortion issues ho sakte hain.

Shadow-Mask Method

Shadow-mask method bhi CRT displays mein use hota hai. Is method mein, ek mask use kiya jata hai jo colors ko alag-alag control karta hai aur accurate image produce karta hai.

Working Principle

  1. Shadow Mask: Display ke andar ek metallic mask hota hai jo pixels ko separate karta hai. Yeh mask holes ki shape mein hota hai.
  2. Color Selection: Jab electron beam shadow mask ke holes ke through pass hoti hai, toh yeh specific phosphor dots par lagti hai, jo colors produce karte hain.
  3. Image Formation: Multiple electron beams (RGB) ko synchronize kiya jata hai taaki image clear aur sharp ban sake.

Advantages

  • Good color reproduction aur accuracy.
  • Lower power consumption compared to beam-penetration method.

Disadvantages

  • Complex manufacturing process.
  • Limited viewing angles due to mask structure.

Input Devices in Computer Graphics

Introduction

Computer graphics mein input devices wo tools hote hain jo user ko computer ko commands dene aur data input karne ki suvidha dete hain. Yeh devices graphical elements ko manipulate karne ke liye zaroori hote hain, jisse design aur visualizations create ki ja sakti hain.

1. Mouse

Mouse ek pointing device hai jo graphical user interface ke sath interact karne ke liye use hota hai. Is device ka istemal cursor ko move karne, selection karne, aur clicking actions perform karne ke liye hota hai.

Features:

  • Pointing Accuracy: Yeh precise pointing aur selection ke liye useful hota hai.
  • Multi-Button Functionality: Zyada buttons hone se alag-alag functions assign kiye ja sakte hain.

2. Graphics Tablet

Graphics tablet ek input device hai jo artists aur designers ke liye specially design kiya gaya hai. Is device par ek stylus ya pen se drawing aur sketching kiya jata hai.

Features:

  • Pressure Sensitivity: Stylus pressure ke anusar lines ki thickness change kar sakta hai.
  • Natural Drawing Experience: Yeh traditional drawing experience ko computer par replicate karta hai.

3. Joystick

Joystick ek input device hai jo primarily gaming aur simulation applications ke liye use hota hai. Is device se user ko directional control aur input milta hai.

Features:

  • 3D Movement: Yeh 3D space mein motion control ke liye ideal hota hai.
  • Analog Input: Joystick smooth control aur response provide karta hai.

4. Scanner

Scanner ek device hai jo physical documents ya images ko digital format mein convert karta hai. Iska use graphics creation aur image processing mein hota hai.

Features:

  • High Resolution: Scanners high-resolution images capture karne ki capability rakhte hain.
  • Versatility: Yeh documents, photos, aur artwork ko digitize karne ke liye useful hote hain.

5. Touchscreen

Touchscreen devices mein users directly screen par touch karke input dete hain. Yeh devices user-friendly interfaces aur applications mein kaam aate hain.

Features:

  • Direct Interaction: Users directly screen par touch karke options choose karte hain.
  • Multi-Touch Capability: Yeh devices multiple touch points ko simultaneously detect karte hain.

1. Random Scan Display

Random Scan Display, jise Vector Display bhi kaha jata hai, ek aisa display hai jo images ko directly draw karne ke liye use hota hai. Ismein electron beam screen par points ya lines ko draw karta hai, jisse shapes ya vector graphics create hote hain.

Working Principle:

  1. Image Data: Image ki data vector format mein hoti hai, jo shapes aur lines ko describe karti hai.
  2. Electron Beam Control: Beam ko specific coordinates par move kiya jata hai aur shapes ko draw karne ke liye activate kiya jata hai.
  3. Refresh Rate: Random scan displays ko images ko refresh karne ki zaroorat nahi hoti, kyunki yeh sirf visible lines aur shapes ko hi draw karte hain.

Advantages:

  • High Quality: High-quality vector graphics produce karte hain.
  • Efficient: Memory ka efficient istemal karte hain kyunki sirf visible parts ko store kiya jata hai.

2. Raster Scan Display

Raster Scan Display ek aisi technique hai jisme images ko pixels ki grid mein arrange kiya jata hai. Is display mein electron beam screen par horizontally left to right scan karta hai aur phir next line par move hota hai.

Working Principle:

  1. Frame Buffer: Raster images ka pixel data frame buffer mein store kiya jata hai.
  2. Scanning Process: Electron beam pixels ko sequentially scan karta hai, top to bottom, left to right.
  3. Refresh Cycle: Ek baar frame complete hone par display ko refresh kiya jata hai.

Advantages:

  • High Resolution: Detailed images ko display kar sakte hain.
  • Color Depth: Multiple colors ko dikhane ki capability rakhte hain.

Computer Graphics and Its Applications

What is Computer Graphics?

Computer Graphics ek aisi technology hai jo computer ke dwara images aur visuals create karne, manipulate karne, aur display karne ke liye use hoti hai. Yeh images 2D ya 3D form mein ho sakti hain. Computer graphics ka istemal kisi bhi digital medium mein hota hai, jaise ki websites, video games, movies, aur presentations.

Key Components of Computer Graphics:

  • Rendering: Images ko generate karna, jo ki textures, colors, aur lighting ke sath hota hai.
  • Modeling: 3D objects ya scenes ko design karna, jo graphics ke visual elements ko create karne ke liye zaroori hai.
  • Animation: Static images ko motion mein convert karna, jisse dynamic visuals milte hain.

Applications of Computer Graphics

Computer Graphics ka istemal har jagah hota hai, chahe wo entertainment ho, education, ya industry. Yahaan kuch major applications diye gaye hain:

1. Entertainment

  • Video Games: High-quality graphics aur immersive experiences ke liye computer graphics ka use hota hai.
  • Movies: Animation aur special effects create karne ke liye CGI (Computer-Generated Imagery) ka istemal kiya jata hai.

2. Education

  • Visual Learning: Educational materials mein illustrations aur diagrams ko samjhne ke liye use kiya jata hai.
  • Simulation: Real-world phenomena ko visualize karne ke liye simulations create kiye jate hain, jaise science experiments ya historical events.

3. Medical Imaging

  • X-rays and MRI: Medical scans ko analyze karne aur diagnose karne ke liye computer graphics ka istemal hota hai.
  • 3D Visualization: Body parts ko visualize karne aur planning surgeries ke liye 3D models banaye jate hain.

4. Design and Manufacturing

  • CAD (Computer-Aided Design): Engineers aur architects designs banane ke liye CAD software ka use karte hain, jo 2D aur 3D modeling mein help karta hai.
  • Prototyping: Products ke prototypes design karne ke liye computer graphics ka use hota hai, jisse physical models banane se pehle visualization kiya ja sake.

CRT Monitors

What is a CRT Monitor?

CRT (Cathode Ray Tube) Monitor ek purana display technology hai jo traditional televisions aur computer monitors mein use hota hai. Is technology mein electron beams ka use karke phosphorescent screen par images create kiye jate hain. Yeh monitors aaj bhi kuch applications mein use hote hain, lekin modern displays jese LCD aur LED ne inhe largely replace kar diya hai.

How Does a CRT Monitor Work?

CRT monitors kaam karte hain ek cathode ray tube ke zariye, jismein electron guns hote hain jo phosphor-coated screen par images create karte hain. Jab electron beams screen par hit karte hain, toh screen par light produce hoti hai, jisse images dikhayi dete hain.

Working Process:

  1. Electron Guns: CRT monitor mein teen electron guns hote hain, jo red, green, aur blue colors ke liye responsible hote hain.
  2. Deflection System: Yeh system electron beams ko control karta hai taaki woh screen par accurately position ho sakein.
  3. Phosphor Coating: Screen par phosphorescent material hota hai jo electron beams ke hit hone par light emit karta hai.
  4. Raster Scanning: Screen ko left se right aur top se bottom scan kiya jata hai, jisse complete image create hoti hai.

Advantages of CRT Monitors

  • Color Reproduction: CRT monitors vibrant colors produce karte hain, jo graphic work aur gaming ke liye beneficial hai.
  • Viewing Angles: Inka wide viewing angle hota hai, jisse images har angle se clearly dekhi ja sakti hain.
  • Response Time: CRT monitors ka response time fast hota hai, jo fast-moving visuals ke liye accha hota hai.

Disadvantages of CRT Monitors

  • Size and Weight: CRT monitors bulky aur heavy hote hain, jo modern slim displays se inferior hain.
  • Power Consumption: Inki power consumption zyada hoti hai, jo efficiency ke liye accha nahi hai.
  • Screen Flickering: Low refresh rates par flickering issue ho sakta hai, jo user experience ko affect karta hai.
